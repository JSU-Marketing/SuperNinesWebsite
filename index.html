The Competitions

Categories

Something for every level — from rising stars to seasoned seniors. The Super Nines has it all.

01

Saint Flannan's College, Camogie

The next generation of hurling stars take centre stage. This competition gives our youngest players a chance to shine in a fast-paced, exciting format that rewards skill and bravery.

  • 9 players per side
  • 20-minute halves
  • Open to players born 2012 or later
  • Club underage panel only
02

Loreto Navan, Ladies Gaelic Football

Rising talents battle it out in the most competitive underage category. Expect skill, determination, and unforgettable moments from the clubs' best young hurlers.

  • 9 players per side
  • 25-minute halves
  • Open to players born 2010 or later
  • Club underage panel only
03

Gort Community College, Men's Hurling

Clubs' junior ranks go head to head in this fiercely contested category. Community pride, local rivalries, and brilliant hurling make this a fan favourite every year.

  • 9 players per side
  • 25-minute halves
  • Club junior grade players only
  • Panel declared 7 days prior
04

CBS Green Tralee, Men's Gaelic Football

The showpiece event. The best senior hurlers clash in a high-octane, winner-takes-all format that delivers drama from throw-in to the final whistle every single time.

  • 9 players per side
  • 30-minute halves
  • Club senior grade players only
  • Panel declared 7 days prior
